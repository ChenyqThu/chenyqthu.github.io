战略调整与重大变动 Strategic Shifts & Major Changes

产品与市场

Product & Market Focus

企业技术平台 (Enterprise Technology) 表现强劲，成为增长核心。分销与直销渠道并行，分别占营收55%和45% (前九个月)。

Strong performance in Enterprise Technology, core growth driver. Balanced channel strategy: 55% distributor, 45% direct sales (9-months).

供应链与库存

Supply Chain & Inventory

曾面临严重供应限制。采取措施增加库存、支付更高成本以保供应。库存和供应商存款曾增加，相关风险仍存。前九个月存货增加1.35亿美元，供应商预付款减少。

Previously faced severe constraints. Measures taken: increased inventory, higher costs for supply. Risks of obsolescence remain. 9-month inventory up $135M, vendor deposits down.

外部风险

External Risks

关税和贸易紧张局势持续影响利润率。地缘政治风险 (俄乌冲突, 台海局势) 对供应链和经济环境构成不确定性。

Tariffs and trade tensions continue to impact margins. Geopolitical risks (Russia-Ukraine, China-Taiwan) create uncertainty for supply chain and economy.
